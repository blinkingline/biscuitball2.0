BISCUITBALL

Soccer Duel · v2.0

Rules

  • You hold a hand of 3 cards. Each turn you replace the card you played.
  • On offense, play a card or Shoot 🎯 (discard a card instead).
  • On defense, guess their card or Block 🛡️ (discard a card; one turn cooldown).
  • Deck: 5x Forward ⬆️, 3x Left ⬅️, 3x Right ➡️.
  • Shoot/Block is only available in the opponent's half.
  • A correct guess is a tackle! Tackle or OOB switches possession.
  • Shot: Roll d12. Must beat the number on the cell.
  • First to 2 goals wins!
