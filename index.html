BISCUITBALL

Soccer Duel · v2.0

Rules

  • Each turn you draw 3 movement cards from your deck.
  • On offense, you have 4 options: your 3 cards or Shoot 🎯.
  • On defense, you have 4 options: guess their card or Block 🛡️.
  • Deck: 5x Forward ⬆️, 3x Left ⬅️, 3x Right ➡️.
  • Shoot/Block is only available in the opponent's half.
  • A correct guess is a tackle! Tackle or OOB switches possession.
  • Shot: Roll d12. Must beat the number on the cell.
  • First to 2 goals wins!
